FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

  • I CAN'T FIND THE LOG IN SESSION?
    CHECK THE MENU. For mobile phone/tablets just tap the menu button at the top left corner of your screen to access the Menu.

  • DO I NEED TO INVITE OTHERS BEFORE I CAN COLLECT MY HARVEST?
    NO! but we would appreciate if you did, cause it quickens the harvest

  • HOW MANY FARMS CAN I PLANT SIMULTANEOUSLY (AT THE SAME TIME)?
    AS MANY AS YOUR CAN AFFORD. Please do not plant more farms than you can afford

  • WHAT IS THE EXACT DURATION BETWEEN PLANTING AND HARVESTING?
    TYPICALLY 3 DAYS, but there is no exact time, you could get your harvest within a few hours, or in 3 days time, so check your dashboard as often as possible.
